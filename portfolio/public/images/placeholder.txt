Place your project images in:
- /public/images/projects/

Example structure:
/public/images/projects/
  ├── spectra-cover.jpg
  ├── spectra-1.jpg
  ├── spectra-2.jpg
  ├── beans-cover.jpg
  ├── aesth-cover.jpg
  └── graph-cover.jpg

Images should be:
- JPG or PNG format
- Cover images: ~1600x1200px recommended
- Gallery images: ~1600x900px recommended
- Optimized for web (< 500KB per image)
